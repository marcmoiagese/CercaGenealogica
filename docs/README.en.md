# CercaGenealògica

[Catalan](https://github.com/marcmoiagese/CercaGenealogica/blob/master/README.md) · [English](https://github.com/marcmoiagese/CercaGenealogica/blob/master/docs/README.en.md) · [Castilian](https://github.com/marcmoiagese/CercaGenealogica/blob/master/docs/README.cast.md) · [French](https://github.com/marcmoiagese/CercaGenealogica/blob/master/docs/README.fr.md) · [Italian](https://github.com/marcmoiagese/CercaGenealogica/blob/master/docs/README.it.md)

**CercaGenealògica** is an open project dedicated to the research, indexing and consultation of historical documentation with genealogical value.

Its goal is to help individuals, families, researchers, archives and contributors transform historical documents into searchable, connected and useful information for reconstructing family and community histories.

## What is this project?

CercaGenealògica was created as a collaborative tool for working with historical sources such as parish books, censuses, civil registers, municipal records, files and other materials that may contain information about people, families, places and relationships.

The project aims to make information more accessible when it is hidden inside scanned documents, handwritten records, old lists or archives that are difficult to search.

## Why is it useful?

Genealogy is not only a list of names and dates. It is also memory, territory, migration, occupations, family ties, social change and local history.

CercaGenealògica aims to help:

- locate people in historical documents;
- connect records with people and families;
- preserve information extracted from documentary sources;
- encourage collaboration between researchers and volunteers;
- give value to local archives and historical documentation;
- build an open and reusable knowledge base.

## What can be found in it?

The project is designed to work with different types of genealogical and documentary information:

- **Archives and documentary collections**, to identify where information comes from.
- **Books and historical documents**, as consultation units.
- **Pages or images**, linked to the original document.
- **Transcribed records**, with names, dates, places and other relevant data.
- **People and family relationships**, when the available information makes it possible to connect records.
- **Surnames and territorial distribution**, to study the presence and evolution of families.
- **Collaboration and review workflows**, to improve data quality.

## An open and collaborative tool

CercaGenealògica is an open project because the preservation of historical memory and access to it work better when they are shared.

The idea is that anyone interested can contribute according to their possibilities: transcribing documents, reviewing data, improving content, contributing sources, suggesting improvements or helping the project grow.

## Project philosophy

This project is based on a few simple ideas:

- Historical documentation should be easier to find and consult.
- Genealogical data should be reviewable, correctable and improvable.
- Original sources are essential for giving confidence to the information.
- Collaboration can turn scattered documents into shared knowledge.
- Family history is also part of the history of towns, territories and communities.

## Who is it for?

CercaGenealògica may be useful for:

- people researching their family history;
- amateur or professional genealogists;
- local history researchers;
- archives and cultural organizations;
- volunteers working on transcription or indexing;
- communities interested in preserving their documentary memory.

## Project status

CercaGenealògica is under active development.

The project evolves as new needs, new document types, improved search tools, review workflows and more powerful ways to connect people, records and sources are added.
